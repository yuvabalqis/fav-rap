THE SINGER

Nathan Fuller (born April 6, 2004), better known by his stage name 1nonly, is a Korean-American rapper from Las Vegas[2] best known for his breakout single "Stay With Me" which was released in 2020 and gained notoriety due to its popularity on the app TikTok. In 2021, he was signed to Warner Records and released the single "Come Thru" featuring Ciscaux & Shady Moon.[2][3] In 2022, 1nonly released his debut EP, "Homesick", supported by singles "Mine" and "Step Back!" the latter a collaboration with SXMPRA.[4][5] The release was followed by Fuller going on tour in 2023.[6] In June 2023, he released the single "Toolie Beam" in collaboration with rapper Pouya.[7][8]
